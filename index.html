Recovery Rules
1
Stop panicking. What's left is enough to work with if you're disciplined.
2
Cut your size in half. Half your normal risk per trade until you're back to even. Your confidence needs small wins, not big swings.
3
Hit small targets. Don't try to recover everything in one trade.
4
One trade per session. Quality beats quantity every time.
5
Log everything. Every trade logged shows you your patterns.
6
Protect at 20% growth. Once up 20%, drop back to conservative sizing.
